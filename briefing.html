There are 2 groups of gangsters struggling for the control of all illegal activities, such as drugdealing, car stealing, prostitution, blackmailing, gambling...
The first is called "The Familia". The Members are snotnosed and just think the law applies to someone else.
The second is "The Gang", a group of bikers, that like to drive choppers and cause trouble.
The third playable fraction is the Police of Nogova. Needless to say what they have to do...

See the Features and how to play !!!

Don't kill and don't die!
It makes no sense...
- to kill civilians, because you can rob them (gangsters) or you get penalty (police)
- to get killed, because you will loose all your cash, weed and weapons (gangsters)
- to throw your money away, because the team with the most money on the bank will win

Crimerates are:


Copkill: 90

Civilian killed: 30

Rob a bank: 30

Steal a car: 10

Blackmail a shop: 10

Abduct a women: 10

Sell drugs: 10

Rob a Civilian: 2

Features and how to play...


How to make money as a gangster:

- Rob civilians
- Build weed farms, harvest and sell your drugs
- Rob ambulances, synthesize the meidcs to Angel Dust (at bar or HQ), Sell it to Junkies
- Steal cars and sell them (if you do not want them for yourself)
- Collect protection money from shops
- Build a bar or a casino where the cash flows
- Build a brothel and collect women to work there
- Do gun running missions for the gunstores
- Rob a Bank
How do cops make money:

- The income depends on the number of gangsters in play
- Freeing Women from the brothels
- Protect shops from the Gangsters
- Burn weed farms
- Do a razzia at gangster bars or casinos
- Secure stolen cars... bring them to the police station-flag
- Arresting gangsters gives you an award that depends on the “crimerate” of the gangster


Other possible actions:

- Gangsters can make women follow. Be creative in using this (not only for collecting your hookers)
- Gangsters can recruit gunmen…
- Buy a fuel canister… to... refuel a car :P… you can put it on the ground and shoot at it as well. Cops can use them to burn down the weed plantations (gangsters can do this too, but it´s not recommended ;) )
- Buy a repairkit… to -erm... guess what...
- Buy better weapons at the gunshops
- Buy cars… if necessary… at your HQ, bar or at the cardealer
- Feign death or surrender (hands up)… the AI will not shoot at you, if you do this
- Hide handguns… if you don´t have another weapon the ai won´t shoot at you
- Call a taxi
- Gangsters can remove police barriers


Other Features:

- The duration of the arrest depends on the crimerate of the gangster (CR in sec)
- Copkillers are marked on the map and it is the highest scored crime you can commit
- If a player dies he looses all his cash, money and additional weapons (you always start with a handgun)… so it might be better to be arrested
- Teams' vehicles respawn at HQs and bases
- Gangsters can not enter police stations
- Showscore-option
- player and team statistics


Cops only:

- Cops can jump from one police station to another at their radio buildings
- Cops have bigger choice of vehicles and weapons… (depending on “global crimerate”)
- Cops don't need cash, they pay with “police-account-card” ;)
- Cops can… well,... arrest and disarm Gangsters
- Cops can build barriers at police cars
- Talk-Menu: they can say short orders, that other players can hear, if they are near. (will be improved)
